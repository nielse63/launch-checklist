City Wilderness Abound

In Riverline, a variety of river-centric ecosystems can be found right in your backyard. A wetland restores the South Bank of the Chicago River to the untamed environment it once was – a natural home for native flora, fauna and migratory birds.

Inspire

Kayak Launch

Explore the South Branch of the Chicago River with the public kayak launch.

Riverwalk

The riverwalk stretches from Harrison Street to Roosevelt Avenue, complete with benches, informational signage, and trails through local vegetation.

Green Space

Riverline’s central green space includes an amphitheater, adventure playground, and multiple ecological zones.

Wetlands

Riverline’s indigenous wetland allows for the sustainable management of storm water runoff, and creates a natural environment for local flora and fauna.

ARCHITECTURE THAT INSPIRES

Luxurious living meets ecologically-conscious design in Riverline, where 10 new buildings from award winning architects Perkins+Will offer sophisticated contemporary style. Best-in-class amenities -- ranging from restorative spa facilities to innovative fitness centers to private club rooms and collaborative working lounges -- offer endless opportunities for inspiration, while environment-friendly and energy efficient details create a bold neighborhood with a sustainable footprint.

Engage

COMMUNITY THAT ENGAGES

A network of trails and bridges connects the neighborhood to the rest of Chicago, allowing Riverline’s multiple communal spaces to become invaluable assets for both residents and visitors alike.

Explore

LOCATION IS PRIME

Optimal for those who want the best of the city within reach, in Riverline, everything is close and nothing intrudes. The neighborhood is just steps from both Downtown and the Lakefront, offering easy access to public transportation and the city’s parks, trails, and museums.
